TIER FAQs

What is an E-Rickshaw?

E-Rickshaw is a service offered by tier to provide eco-friendly and affordable transportation solutions in urban and suburban areas. It involves electric rickshaws that are operated by trained drivers and can be booked through the Tier website.

How do I book an E-Rickshaw on TIER?

To book an Tier E-Rickshaw, follow these simple steps:
a. Download the Tier app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
b. Sign in or create an Tier account.
c. Enter your pickup and drop-off locations.
d. Choose "E-Rickshaw" as your ride type.
e. Confirm your booking, and the nearest available E-Rickshaw will be assigned to you.

What are the benefits of using E-Rickshaws?

E-Rickshaws are eco-friendly, cost-effective, and provide a convenient mode of transportation for short distances.

Are Tier E-Rickshaws safe?

Yes, Tier ensures the safety of its users by partnering with registered and verified drivers for their E-Rickshaws. Drivers undergo a thorough background check and are trained to prioritize passenger safety.

What is the fare structure for Tier E-Rickshaws?

The fare for Tier E-Rickshaws is generally lower compared to traditional auto-rickshaws. The pricing is calculated based on factors such as distance traveled and the time taken. You can check the estimated fare in the Tier website before confirming your booking.

Can I pay with cash for an Tier E-Rickshaw ride?

Tier primarily encourages cashless payments for all their services, including E-Rickshaws. You can pay for your ride using various digital payment methods available in the Tier website, such as credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or UPI.

Can I book an Tier E-Rickshaw for multiple passengers?

Yes, you can book an E-Rickshaw for multiple passengers. However, the capacity of an E-Rickshaw may vary, and it's essential to ensure that the number of passengers does not exceed the permitted limit for safety reasons.

Are Tier E-Rickshaws available 24/7?

Tier E-Rickshaws are generally available 24/7, subject to the availability of drivers in your area. However, the availability may vary based on your location and the time of day.

How can I track my Tier E-Rickshaw ride?

Once you've booked an E-Rickshaw through the Tier website, you can track your ride in real-time using the website's built-in GPS tracking feature. This allows you to know the driver's current location and estimated time of arrival.

Can I cancel my Tier E-Rickshaw booking?

Yes, you can cancel your E-Rickshaw booking through the Tier website. However, cancellation charges may apply if the cancellation is made after a specific time frame.

What should I do if I face any issues during my Tier E-Rickshaw ride?

If you encounter any problems during your E-Rickshaw ride, you can use the Tier website's customer support feature to report the issue or contact Tier's customer support directly for assistance.

For any further inquiries or specific concerns, please feel free to contact Tier's customer support or refer to the help section on their website.
